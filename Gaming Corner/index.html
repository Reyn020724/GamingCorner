Tank Emblem: Maximize durability by prioritizing HP and defense attributes.
Mage Emblem: Increase magic penetration and cooldown reduction for mages.
Assassin Emblem: Prioritize physical penetration and adaptive damage for burst heroes.
Support Emblem: Focus on healing effects and cooldown reduction for team utility.
